Why Switch?

It feels like a 50/50 choice, but your brain is playing tricks on you.

  • The Start: You have a 1/3 chance of picking the car. The other doors have a 2/3 chance.
  • The Reveal: Monty opens a door. His hands are tied—he cannot pick the car. He effectively consolidates that entire 2/3 chance into the one remaining door.

By always switching, you instantly win the car every single time your first pick was wrong!
